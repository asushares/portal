ASU SHARES Consent Portal

A comprehensive platform for managing healthcare consent documents and patient data sharing agreements for Substance Use Disorder (SUD) care.

SHARES (Substance use HeAlth REcord Sharing) is dedicated to improving care for individuals with Substance Use Disorders by enhancing the sharing of medical records between patients and providers. Our mission is to balance patient privacy and data access needs through automated data segmentation methods.
